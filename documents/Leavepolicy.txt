ABC TECHNOLOGIES LEAVE POLICY

1. ANNUAL LEAVE
- Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
- Up to 5 unused days may be carried forward to the following year.
- Leave requests should be submitted at least one week in advance.

2. SICK LEAVE
- Employees are entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave annually.
- A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

3. CASUAL LEAVE
- Employees receive 6 days of casual leave annually.
- Casual leave should be requested in advance whenever possible.

4. MATERNITY LEAVE
- Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.

5. PATERNITY LEAVE
- Male employees are entitled to 10 days of paid paternity leave.

6. BEREAVEMENT LEAVE
- Employees are entitled to 5 days of paid leave in the event of the death of an immediate family member.

7. UNPAID LEAVE
- Extended unpaid leave may be granted subject to manager and HR approval.

8. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
- Employees are entitled to all government-declared public holidays.

9. LEAVE APPROVAL PROCESS
- Leave requests should be submitted through the HR portal.
- Managers are responsible for approving or rejecting requests.
- Emergency leave requests should be communicated immediately to the reporting manager.

10. LEAVE ENCASHMENT
- Unused annual leave beyond the carry-forward limit is not eligible for encashment unless specifically approved by management.
