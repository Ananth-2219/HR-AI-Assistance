ABC TECHNOLOGIES EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK

1. WORKING HOURS
- Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday.
- Employees are entitled to a one-hour lunch break.
- Flexible working arrangements may be approved by managers.

2. ATTENDANCE
- Employees are expected to report to work on time.
- Managers should be informed promptly in case of absence.
- Repeated lateness may result in disciplinary action.

3. REMOTE WORK POLICY
- Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval.
- Employees are expected to be available during core business hours.

4. CODE OF CONDUCT
- Professional behavior is expected at all times.
- Harassment, discrimination, and workplace bullying are strictly prohibited.
- Confidential company information must not be shared externally.

5. PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
- Performance reviews are conducted annually.
- Employees are encouraged to discuss goals and career development with their managers.

6. TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT
- Employees are encouraged to complete at least 20 hours of training annually.
- Approved certifications may be reimbursed by the company.

7. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
- Business-related expenses must be submitted within 30 days.
- Receipts are required for reimbursement.

8. TRAVEL POLICY
- Business travel requires manager approval.
- Economy class should be used for domestic travel.
- Accommodation expenses should comply with company guidelines.

9. DRESS CODE
- Smart casual attire is expected.
- Employees representing the company externally should maintain a professional appearance.

10. HOLIDAYS
- Employees are entitled to all public holidays declared by the government.
- Additional company holidays may be announced periodically.

11. IT SECURITY POLICY
- Employees must use secure passwords.
- Company systems should not be shared with unauthorized individuals.
- Confidential information must be protected.

12. TERMINATION POLICY
- Employees with less than two years of service must provide 30 days notice.
- Employees with more than two years of service must provide 60 days notice.
- Final settlements will be processed within 30 days.
